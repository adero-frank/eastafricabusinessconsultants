What are the requirements for registering a company in Kenya?

The requirements for company registration in Kenya include:

  • Proposed company name (minimum of three options)
  • Details of directors (minimum of one, maximum of twenty)
  • Details of shareholders and their share allocation
  • Physical address of the company's registered office in Kenya
  • Company secretary details (can be appointed after registration)
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association

The entire process typically takes 7-14 working days, depending on the company structure.

How long does it take to register a company in Kenya?

The company registration timeline in Kenya varies based on several factors:

  • Private Company Limited by Shares: 7-10 working days
  • Public Limited Company: 10-14 working days
  • Company Limited by Guarantee: 7-12 working days
  • Foreign Company Registration: 14-21 working days

These timelines assume all required documents are correctly prepared and submitted. Our firm guarantees efficient processing through our established relationships with regulatory bodies.
